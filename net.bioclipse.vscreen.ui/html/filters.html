VScreen Filters

This is a list of the available filters in VScreen.

NameIDDescription
MW net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.MWFilter Filter out substances by molecular weight
XlogP net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.XlogPFilter Filter out substances by partition coefficient (XlogP)
restrictElement net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.RestrictElementFilter Filter out substances by requiring the prescence of one or more elements
RingCount net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.RingCountFilter Filter out substances by number of rings in the structure.
RuleOfFive net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.RuleOfFiveFilter Filter out if above a certain number of failures for Lipinsky Rule Of Five
smarts net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.SMARTSFilter Filter out undesired structures defined by SMARTS
TPSA net.bioclipse.vscreen.filters.TPSAFilter Filter out substances by a speciied topological polar surface area (TPSA).

Example usage of filters

      filters=java.util.ArrayList();
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("XlogP" , "<" , 4));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("XlogP" , ">" , -2));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("MW"    , "<" , 450));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("MW"    , ">" , 150));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("restrictElement", "C,N,O,Cl,S,F"));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("RingCount"    , ">=" , 1));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("RuleOfFive"    , "<=" , 1));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("TPSA"    , "<" , 200));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("TPSA"    , ">" , 80));
      filters.add(vscreen.createFilter("SMARTS"    , "a[N;X2]=O CO[N;X2]=O"));
